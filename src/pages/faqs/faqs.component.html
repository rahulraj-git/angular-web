Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. How long does shipping take within India?

Shipping within India typically takes 5-7 business days, depending on your location.

2. What is your return and exchange policy?

We accept returns or exchanges within 2 days of delivery for non-customized items in their original condition. Customized jerseys cannot be returned or exchanged.

3. Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, we will provide a tracking number via WhatsApp or email for you to track the delivery status.

4. Do you offer cash-on-delivery (COD)?

No, we do not provide COD as our products are fully customized and personalized. If the parcel is not received, it becomes a total loss for us since the items cannot be resold. All orders must be prepaid.

5. What should I do if I receive the wrong or damaged product?

If you receive a wrong or damaged product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery. We will arrange for a replacement or refund after reviewing your complaint.

6. How do I confirm my order?

Once you finalize the design and details, our team will share a proforma invoice. After making the payment, your order will be confirmed, and production will begin.

7. Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Yes, we provide discounts on bulk orders for teams, clubs, and resellers. Contact us on WhatsApp to get a personalized quote.

8. Can I see a sample of my design before production?

Yes, we provide a digital mock-up of your design for approval before production starts.

9. Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide. International delivery times and charges vary based on location. Contact us for details.

10. How do I get in touch with your team for more queries?

You can reach us through WhatsApp or email for any questions or assistance. Our team is happy to help!
